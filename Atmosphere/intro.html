Atmospheric Pressure

Understanding the Pressure of the Air Around Us

Welcome to an interactive exploration of atmospheric pressure — one of the most fundamental concepts in meteorology and physics. Every moment of every day, the atmosphere presses down on us with a force of 14.7 pounds on every square inch of our bodies.

What You'll Discover:
  • The definitions of pressure and atmospheric pressure
  • How pressure is measured via a mercury tube barometer
  • Metric units: Pascal, Atmospheres, bar, and millibar
  • Practical units: psi, millimeters of mercury (mm Hg or Torr) and inches of mercury (in Hg)
