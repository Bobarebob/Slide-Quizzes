Pitot-Static → Altimeter
STATIC PRESSURE ONLY · ALTITUDE MEASUREMENT
Altimeter principle:   Altitude ∝ −ln(Pstatic / Psea-level)  ·  As the aircraft climbs, static pressure falls, and the altimeter needle rotates clockwise.  ·  The Kollsman window lets the pilot dial in the local barometric pressure so the altimeter reads true altitude, not pressure altitude.
