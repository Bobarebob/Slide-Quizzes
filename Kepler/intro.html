Kepler's Laws of Planetary Motion

An Interactive Journey Through the Solar System

🪐

Welcome to an interactive exploration of Johannes Kepler's revolutionary laws of planetary motion. These three fundamental principles transformed our understanding of how planets move through space and laid the foundation for modern astronomy.

What You'll Discover:
  • The life and work of Johannes Kepler, one of history's greatest astronomers
  • How planets move in elliptical orbits around the Sun
  • Why planets move faster when closer to the Sun
  • The mathematical relationship between orbital period and distance
  • Interactive visualizations that bring these laws to life

Each section includes detailed explanations, interactive animations, and quiz questions to test your understanding. Take your time exploring each law and experimenting with the visualizations.
